Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Kavach ("we", "our", or "us") is a security deception platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and hashed password when you create an account.
  • Honeypot Trigger Data: IP addresses, user agents, request headers, and access patterns of entities that interact with your deployed honeypot tokens.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and timestamps for improving our service.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device fingerprints for security classification purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Kavach security deception service.
  • To detect, classify, and profile potential attackers who trigger your honeypot tokens.
  • To send you security alerts and notifications about detected threats.
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To improve our threat detection algorithms and classification models.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with appropriate cost factors.
  • All API communications are encrypted via TLS.
  • JWT tokens expire after 7 days and are stored in HTTP-only cookies.
  • Database access is restricted and encrypted at rest.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Attacker profile data and trigger event logs are retained for 90 days by default, unless you configure a different retention period in your settings.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregate threat intelligence data to improve collective security. If you configure webhook or Slack integrations, alert data is sent to those endpoints under your control.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

8. Cookies

We use a single essential cookie ("token") to maintain your authenticated session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics pixels.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@kavach.security.
