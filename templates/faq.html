Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KAVACH

🔍
How long does it take to deploy KAVACH?
▼
KAVACH deploys in 5 minutes. Just pull the Docker container, set environment variables, and start. No complex setup. No code changes needed in your application.
Do I need to modify my existing code?
▼
No. KAVACH sits as a reverse proxy layer before your application. It intercepts traffic without modifying your code. Your application runs unchanged.
Can I use KAVACH with my existing security tools?
▼
Yes. KAVACH integrates via webhooks with any SIEM, ticketing system, or monitoring tool. Slack, Teams, PagerDuty, Splunk, ELK — all supported.
What types of honeypot tokens can I create?
▼
Five types: API Keys (fake credentials), URLs (embedded in code/docs), Documents (watermarked files), DNS Records (monitoring subdomains), and Email Addresses (mailing lists). Mix and match based on your needs.
How many tokens can I create?
▼
Starter plan: 10 tokens. Professional: 50 tokens. Enterprise: Unlimited. No overage charges — extra tokens cost $50 each or upgrade your plan.
Can attackers tell if a token is fake?
▼
No. KAVACH tokens are indistinguishable from real credentials. They follow your exact naming conventions, formats, and encoding. Attackers have no way to know until they're blocked.
How quickly do I get alerted when a token is triggered?
▼
Instantly. Detection is <100ms. Alert delivery to Slack/webhook/email is real-time (typically 1-5 seconds). See attacks as they happen.
Will I get false positives?
▼
No. Zero false positives. Honeypot = definite attack. Unlike traditional WAFs that flag suspicious traffic, KAVACH only alerts on confirmed threat activities.
Can I customize alert rules?
▼
Yes. Set risk thresholds, alert channels per token type, quiet hours, and escalation rules. Route critical alerts to phone, low-risk to Slack.
Can I switch plans anytime?
▼
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade instantly. We prorate charges based on usage. No cancellation fees.
Do you offer discounts?
▼
Yes. Annual billing included above pricing. 2+ year commitments: 20% discount. Non-profit/government: 30% discount. Contact sales for custom terms.
What if I need more than my plan allows?
▼
Add-ons available: Extra tokens ($50/token), additional users ($100/user/month), premium support ($500/month). Or upgrade to the next tier.
Is my data encrypted?
▼
All data in transit uses TLS 1.3. At rest: AES-256 encryption. Self-hosted: full control. Cloud: managed encryption keys with option for customer-managed keys.
Do you meet compliance standards?
▼
Yes. SOC 2 Type II certified. GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS compliant. Full audit trails. Automated compliance reporting for your auditors.
Can I self-host KAVACH?
▼
Yes. KAVACH is available as a self-hosted Docker image. Full control of data. On-premise or private cloud. Enterprise plans include this by default.
